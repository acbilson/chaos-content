Our family visited my grandparents last week. We managed the travel by splitting it into two five-hour drives with hotel pool swimming in-between.

Our first full day we drove down from Meeker to Glenwood Springs and spent three hours swimming in the hot springs. The second day, after seeing how enthralled the boys were with exploring my grandparent’s 10-acre property, playing with my childhood toys and riding the ATV, we kept to the house, with only a brief jaunt into town to survey the homes I’d lived in growing up.
